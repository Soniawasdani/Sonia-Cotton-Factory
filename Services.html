    WHAT WE DO

    We deal with the exports and local handling of raw cotton. We have our own ginning facilities in the prime quality raw cotton producing areas of Pakistan. We have sold Pakistani raw cotton in our region (Pakistan), and to some of our valued buyers in Bangladesh, Indonesia, Thailand, Philippines, Taiwan, and Vietnarn.

    OUR EDGE

    The key to quality production is quality material. Our company has a unique advantage of supplying cotton from its own production site and also has cotton-testing facilities. This enables us to provide highly regular and uniform cotton to our clients. In addition, we have our sale/ purchase offices located throughout Pakistan, to assure even and smooth supplies to our clients. We have exported directly and in association with our concerns, Our exports are growing every day with the entire satisfaction of our valued buyers. Our Marketing team is comprised of most experienced and highly educated marketers well exposed to the International Cotton Market and is in good contacts with their long marketing background. All of the people those who are dealing with the International Market are the professionals and have used to be the top ranking officials in the Multinational Companies. We always try at our best to ship worth of cost and the best available stuff to our valued buyers in order to establish a good and long term business relation. None of us is interested in transactional business. All the efforts are made to avoid any un-pleasant happenings but in case it is taken as the first priority to make a remedy with the help of agency and in our experience of decades it is always solved before moving towards arbitration.

    WE BREATH QUALITY

    The Cotton manufactured by us is the Ultimate Hallmark of the finest quality and lifelong value since:
    • We have the best and latest manufacturing infrastructure with our own research and development laboratory to give the best quality cotton to our customers. To keep Cotton contamination free, we have sensible and practical storage system.
    • Our Raw Cotton Heaps are of 1000MT, which enables interruption free quality production. Ample covered space for finished goods to keep it free from the effects of the atmosphere.
    Our cotton travels overseas safely because it is factory stuffed.
